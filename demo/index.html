Music Generation

CS 221 Final Project (2017) - Akash Mahajan, Suraj Heereguppe, Nathan Dalal

Baseline

We want to use the MIDI file format to generate good music.
As a decent baseline, pink noise samples inversely by frequency to create better than random noise.

Markov Models

We learned note transitions from songs and used transition probabilities to generate notes.
We also tried incorporating duration into our Markov Models.

Recurrent Neural Networks

We represented notes as one-hot vectors and let recurrent networks learn trends and get creative!
